UMAW hosting a panel 10/8 on "Sync Rights, Publishing Splits, Who owns your Master recordings, and more!"

A cool educational event that's both in person (NYC) and remote/zoom. You can register here

from an email:


The Rising Artist Foundation
and UMAW are excited to announce the second Office Hours event on October 8th in NYC. Office hours is our coast-to-coast monthly educational series where a panel present honest information on how to navigate the music industry. For independent and emerging artists without a thoughtful team, this knowledge is hard to find and often harder to understand. We hope to bridge that gap and help working artists thrive.

Sign up here and together we can share what we know, learn, and make the music industry a place all of us can thrive!

Join us for the first event of this series at Performance Space in New York City on October 8th from 7-9pm to discuss and learn all about what you need to know for: Sync Rights, Publishing Splits, Who owns your Master recordings, and more!

Panelists: Mike Lawrence (Music Lawyer), Shilpa Ray (Musician), Zack Weinstein (Publishing), moderated by Joey DeFrancesco (La Neve, Downtown Boys)
​
Sessions will be recorded and we will be building public resources (budget templates, glossaries, etc.) to help put theory into practice and to create a growing set of resources.

Come ask your burning questions and meet other creatives.

Let's put theory into practice!

Solidarity,

UMAW
