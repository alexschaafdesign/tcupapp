WILD DYL & A SHARPENED WHISPER —
"HONEY, I MISCHANNELED THE ORACLE" (2018–2024)
ASW–001
available on cd & digital formats at sharpwhisper.bandcamp.com

"HONEY, I MISCHANNELED THE ORACLE" (2018—2024) contains themes of naive spirituality, sexual mysticism, & delusional thinking. It's about the hilarious, humiliating indifference of the natural world. This is a formalist work set in four interlocking triptychs & two asymmetrical halves, totaling 12 tracks in 37 minutes. Composed, improvised, recorded, & compiled by WILD DYL, with psychic assistance from A SHARPENED WHISPER.
Click to expand...

I self-released a record this year. It’s available on a beautiful cd edition – a silver, glass-mastered, archival-quality compact disc housed in a four-panel digipack with original color photography. It's usually in stock at Electric Fetus, in the electronic CDs, under W for Wild Dyl, but I think I need to deliver more copies. Of course, a digital edition is also available on bandcamp.

Records that inspired me to make this (musically, conceptually, and visually) include:
  • Comme un seul narcisse (2016) by Felicia Atkinson & Jefre Cantu-Ledesma
  • As Falls Wichita, So Falls Wichita Falls (1980) by Pat Metheny & Lyle Mays
  • Zuckerzeit (1974) by Cluster
  • Plux Quba (1988) by Nuno Canavarro
  • Grid of Points (2018) by Grouper
Thanks for considering... Happy new year ahead!
Dylan
