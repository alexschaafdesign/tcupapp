We are in a similar situation with a new folk/Americana/country duo. We've been emailing 30 or so venues and filling up the calendar that way, but here are a few things you might try besides cold emails to bookers:
- Finnish bistro on Como - they book anything, you go to the website and request a date on the calendar. They give you 15% of food and drink sales during your performance.

56 brewing in NE - they have an open mic once a month hosted by the booker. We got a gig playing a few songs then introducing ourselves. They book solo stuff on Sundays

Sociable ciderwerks in NE - they have an open mic that usually had above avg musicians so it's good to meet people. Jt who runs the open mic books a showcase each week where performers from the open mic do longer sets and get paid some amount of money.
