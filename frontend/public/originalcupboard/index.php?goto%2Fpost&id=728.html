Hello! I’m Nathan Johnston (he/they) and I love our music scene so stinkin much. By day I am a personal banker/financial advisor. Feel free to reach out if you need human support for banking stuff. By night I’m a bassist and have gotten a lot of love for my videography efforts. I post too many band videos on IG as @mnhoneypants and @banginrandos and am a regular contributor to UndercurrentMPLS.

I’ve been living back in the twin cities for a couple of years now and I get so much joy from connecting with and supporting our arts scene. I grew up in Burnsville and worked at the garage for a some years. I spent a decade down in Florida playing a lot of blues music. Lately very into weird music that gets me dancing. Every time I feel like I am familiar with ‘the scene’ I get introduced to a new band and a whole new bubble of art to explore. I am full of gratitude to get to witness some of your efforts.

Send me a show invite if you’d like some videos of a gig. I’m happy to post one or just send you some footage for use at your discretion. If my schedule is clear I would be happy to catch a show and come cheer you on.

Hello all! This place seems great, super happy I found it and grateful to all who have built it up to this point. I'm John, have been living in the Twin Cities this latest go-round for 8 years now, and have been writing, recording, and performing (but rarely releasing) music as Aloysius the Band for the better part of 17 years, with some Very Large Gaps in there. Currently I'm working on self-recording an album of what I've been calling "cosmic indie folk" for lack of a better sense of my own genre, and playing out if friends ask me to open for them. I'm mainly a guitarist, but am confidently medium at several different things including bass, drums, and synth/keys things, and I'm always looking for new bandmates (lately it's been always an Aloysius, never a Band ) and/or to participate in the artistry of others.

Also I started DIYing my own effects pedals, which has been super rewarding, and I too enjoy traipsing on the local trails with my family (we went to the Eloise Butler Wildlife Sanctuary a couple weeks back, it was real nice). Hope to meet you all out there!

Hi all!
My name is Sabrina Rose. Transplant from Northwestern WI, living in the Apple Valley area for three years as of this year!

Hey ya’ll! I’m Tyler Steinley, and currently reside in Winona, MN. I’ve been peripherally involved in the Twin Cities scene since 2019 through drumming with my bands Sleeping Jesus and Afflatus. I also produce under the name “Oof”. I’ve already met a lot of you through those avenues, plus working sound at the local Winona spot - No Name Bar.

It’s so inspiring to see community members organize like this, and I’m very excited to be involved from afar. Although the Winona music scene has been reduced a lot post-COVID, we still have a lot of amazing people, places, and events in this small town. Please don’t hesitate to reach out if you’re trying to make a trip down river for a show or collaborations!

My current plan is actually to be moving to ya’ll’s neck of the woods in June - where I’m hoping to get involved much more with TCUP, drum/produce as much as possible, and hopefully find work being a sound engineer as often as I can. I’m already so grateful for this community as it seems like this will be the best starting point for making connections in line with all those goals!

See you out there!
