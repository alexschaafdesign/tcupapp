Hello,

Back in November, I booked a show for The Del-Viles at Terminal Bar for this coming April. I suggested specific dates, and they got back to me and confirmed a specific date worked. After this they suggested that I send bios for each band, a press photo, and links to social media. There was no language specifying that the confirmation of the booking date was contingent on me providing these supplementary materials. After I confirmed everything with the two other bands I wanted to play with, I sent the Terminal booker an email with links to all bands' social media stating I would include bios and press photos as soon as I had them. After I sent this email, the booker said "sounds great, thank you!" Then I received another email stating that I was in negotiation for a date at Terminal Bar and that I was to supply band promotional materials in order to change the calendar booking from "yellow" to "green," green indicating fully confirmed. No mention of a booking calendar was made until this email. I checked their newly available booking calendar, and other bands were booked for the date I asked for. I followed up asking for clarification: is confirmation of the booking date contingent on providing promotional materials? I have not received an answer. I wanted to share this information with Cupboard--I was fond of playing at the Terminal and we have played I believe 3 shows there previously, but I had not encountered this until a couple of days ago. I'm not discouraging anyone from playing there, but I did want to bring this confusing set of emails to Cupboard's attention. I will include screenshots of the conversation. I had to cancel the show with the other two bands. Thanks--
