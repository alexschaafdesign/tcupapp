Hey everybody,

I wanted to make sure you all know about Brass Solidarity - we’re a street activist band based out of George Floyd Square. We will show up for events that fit our values when requested from our website. Generally we want two weeks’ notice for events, but can sometimes put a group together with a quick turnaround because of a specific event (for instance, we have quick turnarounds when playing for vigils).

We’ve played with various teachers’ unions in town, for the east phillips protest events, and dozens of other orgs around the cities.

Let us know if we can support your protest/event!
