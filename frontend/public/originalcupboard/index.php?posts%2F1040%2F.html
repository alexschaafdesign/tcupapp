Hey all, nice to see all these familiar names and faces here, I've already spotted a few recent and upcoming collaborators!

We are a local live music streaming show based in Fridley. We stream, film, record and edit 20-30 minute shows consisting of 3-6 songs per episode/band. I could really ramble on about the project, but I would just recommend you to check out one of our recent videos over on our YouTube channel. Under the "videos" tab are the fully produced, mixed, and edited tracks, but you can also check out replays of our live shows under the "live" tab.

Our main mission is to provide quality photos, videos, and recordings to independent local musicians. We're entirely self-produced, and so we have a lot of creative freedom. However, we're beginning to more seriously seek out sponsors, donors, or grants. Ultimately, we would like to pay our guests, our crew, and ourselves.

We've been interested in TCUP since learning about them earlier in the year, and found the forum as a result of an internal discussion about finding an alternative to Meta products (I don't think we're ready to abandon Instagram just yet, but hoping and looking for better options...) and I was so excited that this forum already exists! We'll try to be active users here.

I have added a little bit more detail in our About Us section of our profile. Or hit the links in the signature for our IG or website, and feel free to reach out directly!
